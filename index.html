Bio

Inspired by the rock classics of the 70s, Hunted by Elephants are a high-energy 4-piece hard rock band renewing the time-honoured union of soaring vocals, growling riffs and energising drumming.

Formed in mid-2018 by Tolis (vocals), Roman (guitar) and Ray (bass), and now joined by Alex (drums). Riff driven songs have heavy undertones and invoke an atmosphere of the golden years of hard rock from the 70s and beyond.
